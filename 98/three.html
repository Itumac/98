Three Degrees Kelvin

The warm embrace of God
is three degrees Kelvin
The only sign he wanted love at all.

Such cold arms--like being held by a corpse,
I tossed restlessly and his withered arms snapped away.

I am free now and as warm as I need to be
Three degrees Kelvin.

-Tim Camuti 11/98
